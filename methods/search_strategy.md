# RSV Evidence Map Search Strategy

## Automated source groups

- PubMed / NCBI E-utilities
- medRxiv
- ClinicalTrials.gov
- Crossref DOI metadata
- CDC / ACIP guidance pages
- FDA regulatory and safety pages
- WHO guidance pages
- EMA regulatory pages
- PHAC / NACI guidance pages
- UKHSA / JCVI guidance pages

## Core RSV prevention concepts

The automated surveillance focuses on RSV evidence related to immunization, vaccination, monoclonal antibodies, maternal protection, infant protection, adult vaccination, vaccine efficacy/effectiveness, safety, implementation, and immune correlates.

## Important limitation

Auto-added records are algorithmically classified and not manually verified.
